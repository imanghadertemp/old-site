Icebrg is an app concept that challenges the way we currently go about organizing hangouts. Various tools exist to connect us with new people, while Icebrg focuses on gaining a broader, more holistic view of our existing network of friends. By searching an activity, Icebrg shows you the friends who have this interest, or something related to it, listed under their profile. This allows you to filter and invite friends you already know would be interested.

During an eight hour rapid prototyping session, the Icebrg team drew together the prototype shown above. Starting with research into the user's paint points, we began to discuss solutions to each problem with the current way hangouts are organized.

This app is the product of newly launched startup team, Icebrg, based in Ottawa, ON. All concepts, and executions are owned by the Icebrg team.
